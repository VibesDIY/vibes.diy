# lonely-socks-club

> Build a playful social app called Lonely Socks Club where people post photos of single lonely socks searching for their soulmate after getting separated in the laundry. The emotional tone should parody dramatic dating apps and heartfelt reunion stories. Every sock should feel like a tiny abandoned character with hopes and dreams. The app should encourage users to upload photos of unmatched socks, give them funny names, write exaggerated bios, and browse possible matches from nearby dorms, apartments, or friend groups. When two socks are reunited, the app should celebrate with absurd emotional language like a reality TV dating finale or a wedding announcement. The UI should feel warm, funny, and slightly overdramatic, like a combination of a dating app, a pet adoption page, and a support group for laundry tragedy survivors. Encourage sharing, reactions, comments, and dramatic AI-generated narration about the loneliness of the sock and its quest for completion. The whole experience should feel low stakes, communal, weirdly wholesome, and immediately understandable to exhausted college students doing laundry at 1am. $STYLE

Live at [https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/lonely-socks-club](https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/lonely-socks-club)

## Run it

```sh
npx vibes-diy push     # uploads App.jsx, prints a live HTTPS URL
```

Edit [App.jsx](App.jsx) and push again to iterate.

- `npx vibes-diy push` — deploy the current directory
- `npx vibes-diy push --instant-join` — deploy with auto-accept sharing
