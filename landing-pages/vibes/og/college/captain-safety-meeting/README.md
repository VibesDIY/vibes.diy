# captain-safety-meeting

> Build a lightweight coordination app called Captain Safety Meeting that helps friend groups find the best overlapping time for spontaneous hangouts, late-night smoke sessions, rooftop chats, game breaks, or other vaguely-defined college rituals. The joke is that everyone knows what a safety meeting means but nobody says it out loud directly. The app should feel casual, funny, slightly conspiratorial, and extremely frictionless. Users should be able to quickly mark when they are free without creating a complicated schedule. The system should feel like a magical social coordinator that instantly figures out the best moment for the crew to assemble. The visual tone should feel like dorm-room logistics meets pirate captain energy meets student-group chaos. Include playful language about assembling the crew, syncing the squad, finding the sacred overlap, or getting the gang together before someone falls asleep. The app should make group scheduling feel social and fun instead of corporate and annoying. Avoid anything that feels like a work calendar or enterprise scheduling software. $STYLE
