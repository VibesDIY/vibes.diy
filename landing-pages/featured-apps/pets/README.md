# Pets

For the animals in your life — coordinate dog walks, log neighborhood cats, and keep tabs on the four-legged regulars.

## Apps

- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/press-tie-difference Dog walker coordinator — schedule group walks; share routes; doggy playdate matching; photo highlights.
- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/cat-spotter-map Neighborhood cat tracker — photo log; vote same/new cat; cluster sightings; simple map.
- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/paw-patrol-planner Dog walker coordinator — schedule group walks; share routes; doggy playdate matching; photo highlights.
- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/pet-feeding-log Pet feeding log — track meals, portions, and treats; share between household members; spot missed feedings.
