# Outdoor Adventure Logs

Log finds in the wild — photo timelines, location-pinned sightings, and shared field notes from the world outside.

## Apps

- https://vibes.diy/vibe/garden-gnome/sunset-timelapse Daily sunset timelapse — daily photo prompt; align frames; auto timelapse; compare dates/angles.
- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/strip-busy-when Neighborhood sound map — record ambient audio at locations; pin to map; curated listening walks; seasonal comparisons.
- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/golden-hour-archive Daily sunset timelapse — daily photo prompt; align frames; auto timelapse; compare dates/angles.
- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/night-sky-journal Stargazing log — point phone at sky; identify constellations; log sightings with friends; track seasonal changes.
- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/cache-quest-journal Geocache adventure log — coordinates and hints; find-and-sign flow; photo proof; difficulty/terrain ratings; personal stats.
