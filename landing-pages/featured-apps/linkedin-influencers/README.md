# LinkedIn Influencers

Cringe-corporate parody generators — humblebrags, hashtag floods, fake CEO quotes, and 10-part threads from a single thought. Paste, generate, post (or don't).

## Apps

- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/li-cliffhanger-writer Cliffhanger post writer — turns a work anecdote into a melodramatic "🧵 1/" thread with line breaks every sentence.
- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/li-hashtag-overlord Hashtag overlord — appends 30+ cringe LinkedIn hashtags to any text (#leadership #grindset #monday).
- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/li-profile-roaster Profile roaster — paste a LinkedIn profile, get a parody "thought leader" rewrite with absurd accomplishments.
- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/li-quote-attributor Fake CEO quote attributor — attributes any sentence to a made-up Fortune-500 CEO with a parody company.
- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/li-til-generator TIL generator — turns a mundane fact into a self-important "Today I Learned" post with buzzword spin.
- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/li-connection-opener Connection opener — generates 5 over-the-top suck-up opening lines from a target's headline.
- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/li-why-i-left "Why I left X" generator — melodramatic parody resignation post for any company.
- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/li-comment-syco Sycophant comment generator — five over-supportive parody replies to any post ("THIS. So much THIS.").
- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/li-title-escalator Job title escalator — turns a real title into 5 absurd influencer titles ("Chief Vibe Architect").
- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/li-engagement-bait Engagement bait generator — 5 cringe questions ending in "Agree? 👇" or "Thoughts?".
- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/li-humblebrag Humblebrag generator — 5 parody humblebrag posts about a real achievement ("Not gonna lie, I almost cried...").
- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/li-thread-stretcher Thread stretcher — expands one sentence into a 10-part numbered LinkedIn thread with motivational filler.
