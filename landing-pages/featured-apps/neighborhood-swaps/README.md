# Neighborhood Swaps & Lists

Community circulation — post what you have, claim what you need, keep stuff moving locally instead of into the landfill.

## Apps

- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/trading-sign-up-list Side-kits trading sign-up list — post items you have/want; claim/borrow flow; simple availability status; notifications for matches.
- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/free-bots-swap Neighborhood free-bots swap — geo feed of free items; claimed/pending states; pickup notes; lightweight moderation.
- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/thread-exchange Clothing swapper — upload pics; tag size/brand; filter by fit; swap requests; meetup coordination.
- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/freebot-swap-hub Neighborhood free-bots swap — geo feed of free items; “claimed/pending” states; pickup notes; lightweight moderation.
- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/side-kits-trading-list Side-kits trading sign-up list — post items you have/want; claim/borrow flow; simple availability status; notifications for matches.
- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/community-freebot-board Neighborhood free-bots swap — geo feed of free items; “claimed/pending” states; pickup notes; lightweight moderation.
- https://vibes.diy/vibe/jchris/closet-exchange Clothing swapper (size + photos) — upload pics; tag size/brand; filter by fit; swap requests; meetup coordination.
