# Vibes DIY Code of Conduct

### **Our Commitment**

Vibes DIY is an open source community for building, learning, and sharing together.

We believe the best work happens in spaces where people feel safe, respected, and encouraged to participate fully.

We are committed to creating a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone regardless of background, identity, experience level, or technical perspective. This Code of Conduct exists to support that goal and to help us take care of one another as a community.

### **Our Expectations**

We ask all community members, contributors, and participants to help create a positive environment by:

* Treating others with respect, kindness, and consideration

* Communicating thoughtfully, even when you disagree

* Giving and receiving feedback in good faith

* Staying focused on what is best for the community and the project

* Respecting differing viewpoints, lived experiences, and technical approaches

* Helping make Vibes DIY a place where people feel comfortable learning and contributing

### **Unacceptable Behavior**

To be clear, the following behaviors are not acceptable in any Vibes DIY community space:

* Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech of any kind

* Personal attacks, insults, or sustained hostility

* Sexualized language or imagery, or other inappropriate content

* Trolling, bad-faith participation, or deliberately disruptive behavior

* Spamming, excessive self-promotion, or persistent off-topic noise

* Impersonation or misrepresentation of identity or affiliation

* Sharing malicious code, exploits, or links intended to harm others

### **Scope**

This Code of Conduct applies to all Vibes DIY community spaces, including but not limited to:

* GitHub repositories, issues, pull requests, and discussions

* Discord servers and channels

* Community events, demos, meetups, and discussions (online and in person)

* Any other spaces where you are representing or participating in the Vibes DIY community

### **Enforcement**

Vibes DIY maintainers and moderators are responsible for upholding this Code of Conduct and supporting the health of the community.

If a violation occurs, they may take actions they believe are appropriate to the situation, including:

* Offering guidance or issuing a warning

* Removing comments, commits, or other content

* Temporarily or permanently restricting participation

* Locking threads or closing issues or pull requests

Enforcement decisions are made with care and at the discretion of the Vibes DIY team and maintainers. Serious or repeated violations may also result in account action under the Vibes DIY Terms of Service.

### **Reporting & Support**

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, we encourage you to reach out to the Vibes DIY maintainers and moderators at help@vibes.diy.

All reports will be reviewed thoughtfully and handled as confidentially as possible. Our goal is not just enforcement, but care. We strive to ensure people feel heard, supported, and safe participating in the community.

### We value your time, your work, and your presence here. Thank you for helping make Vibes DIY a welcoming place for everyone.
